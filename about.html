Smart Street Lights

We through this project are trying to change the way street lights are functioning by reducing the usage of electricity and man power. We designed it in such a way that it only turns on during night. And also it would not be on the whole night it will only switch on when there is a passenger or a pedestrian is going on the street!


ThingSpeak

We used ThingSpeak as our cloud to store,update and control the street lights. We also plotted the stored data to graphs using Matlab functionality.


NodeMCU ESP8266

NodeMCU is a low cost WiFi module we used in our prototype to establish connection to the internet.


LDR Sensor

We used LDR sensor to detect whether it is day or night. It generates variable resistance according to the photons it absorbs until a threshold limit.
